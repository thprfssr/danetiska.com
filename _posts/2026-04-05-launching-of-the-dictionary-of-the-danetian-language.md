The Danetian Academy has launched the
[Dictionary of the Danetian Language](/dictionary)
(*Dictzionariom Danetiskas Dyndjuas*).
This new online
tool makes the lexicon of the Danetian language searchable and accessible to
everyone. The dictionary will be updated periodically as new words are coined
and incorporated into the language.

In addition, the Academy has introduced a Word of the Day feature, highlighting
a selected lemma each day. This provides a simple way to engage with the
language and expand one's vocabulary.

In celebration of the [ten year anniversary](/posts/new-year-3352) of the Danetian
language, the Academy remains committed to further developing and enriching
the Danetian language, and its presentation to the public.
