This site presents a fictional cultural-linguistic project, developed as a form
of long-form
[worldbuilding](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Worldbuilding).
Danetia, the Danetian Academy, and associated
institutions are constructed frameworks used to explore
[language creation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constructed_language),
[epic tradition](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Epic_poetry),
and institutional history.

Created and maintained by Javier Castro.
