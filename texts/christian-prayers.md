En nymeni Pytres, i Sunues, i Sjuntosio Prumenes. Amen.

> In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit. Amen.

Dive, milotios bu medjo, skyltrei.

> O God, be merciful to me, a sinner.

Poti, Jesu Christe, Sunu Divosio, rodi prysjtiom Nai Priskas Tvoias Mytres i
visjom sjuntom, mileioste nos. Amen.

> O Lord, Jesus Christ, Son of God, for the sake of the prayers of Thy Most Pure
Mother and of all the saints, have mercy on us. Amen.

Sjlevos Tebio, Dive nosje, sjlevos Tebio.

> Glory to Thee, our God, glory to Thee.

Redje Divie, Opivoposte, Prumyn Verotas, Jo visjodi esi i visja plyresi,
Strumyn dybrom i bivas Dotor, pribe i dymeie en nysu, ide priskeie nos apo
visjad disjtrad, ide spasje, Dybre, nosja blemyna.

> O Heavenly King, Comforter, Spirit of Truth, Who art everywhere present and
fillest all things, Treasury of good things and Giver of life, come and dwell
in us, and cleanse us of all impurity, and save our souls, O Good One.

Sjunte Dive, Sjunte Myktie, Sjunte Desjmerti, mileioste nos. (Triplysje).

> Holy God, Holy Mighty, Holy Immortal, have mercy on us. (Thrice).

Sjlevos Pytrei, i Sunuei, i Sjuntoi Prumenei, i nuni, i jevi, i en oiva oivom.
Amen.

> Glory to the Father, and to the Son, and to the Holy Spirit, now and ever, and
unto the ages. Amen.

Nai Sjuntu Troiku, mileioste nos. Poti, priskeie skeltins nosjans. Uperpoti,
apojek derysjtans nosjans. Sjunte, opispesje i jubeie nemektins nosjans, rodi
Tvoiosio nymens.

> O Most Holy Trinity, have mercy on us. O Lord, blot out our sins. O Master,
pardon our iniquities. O Holy One, visit and heal our infirmities, for Thy
name's sake.

Poti, mileioste. (Triplysje).

> Lord, have mercy. (Thrice).

Sjlevos Pytrei, i Sunuei, i Sjuntoi Prumenei, i nuni, i jevi, i en oiva oivom.
Amen.

> Glory to the Father, and to the Son, and to the Holy Spirit, now and ever, and
unto the ages. Amen.

Pater Nosje, Jo esi en diusu,
sjunteiostoito nomyn Tvoiom;
opibiet resjmyn Tvoiom;
mysjito velia Tvoia tasme en diuvi i ana djymi;
sjdrynom nosjom anasontiom do nysmos sjadinam;
ide apojek nysmos delgons nosjons jasme i vi apoikmos delgetrymos nosjomos;
ide ne nos endukies en peritim, nu ludreie nos apo dusbytod.

> Our Father, Who art in the heavens, hallowed be Thy name;
Thy kingdom come; Thy will be done on earth as it is in heaven;
Give us this day our daily bread; and forgive us our debts as we
forgive our debtors; and lead us not into temptation, but deliver
us from the evil one.
