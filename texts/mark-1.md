# Marcos

(1:1) Praman evangeliosio Jesues Christosio, Sunues Divosio. (1:2) Yosme pisjto en Prophetasu: «[Behold], proyekmi [messenger] anti tvoyom pretiops, yo iti preparants Tvoyom pontom anti teve. (1:3) Vops udvopomnosio en cjoli: "Prepara pontom Peties, rodjeye Enosio pontons"».

(1:4) Johanna opibemt baptizonts en cjoli [preaching] pe baptismom [of repentance] pro udyektis skaltiom. (1:5) Toda visja dedjom Judaia i toi apo Jerusalems udbament enosmoi da baptizostonto enosme en danui Jordani, udveponts svoyans skeltins.

(1:6) Nui Johanna vestieto en [camel's hair] sjom [leather belt] peri enosio [waist], da edt [locusts and wild honey]. (1:7) Da [he preached], veponts: «Opiyiti Oinos posti mene yo esti [mightier] apo med, yoteram [sandal strap] ne esmi [worthy to stoop down and loose]. (1:8) Veram, vos baptizom sjom udre, nu Eno iti vos baptizonts sjom Sjvente Prumene».
